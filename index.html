How to Play

Instructions for game setup

  1. Enter username
  2. Wait for a friend to enter in their username.
  3. Get your friend's username by texting them.
  4. Enter your friend's name into the opponent box.
  5. Note: Only one friend needs to enter into the opponent box.
  6. A game will now be initiated between you.
  7. For testing purposes, just open two tabs and create two users
  8. Note: Do not click a hand until you have selected an opponent.
  9. If there are errors, both of you should refresh the page and retry from step 1.
